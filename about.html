ABOUT US

Bart Brusselaers and Ivana Srsen, founders of AXENT Architects, are active in architecture since 1986.
They have acquired extensive professional experience by working in renowned architectural firms in
Brussels, in Flanders and in G-D-Luxembourg.

CREATION OF AXENT

In 2005, they chose to create their own company AXENT. Gradually, their know-how developed and
was refined by taking advantage of the experience of a large office and applying it to a more efficient size.
